## What is Waymark?

Waymark is an iOS prayer tracking and management application that helps Christians:

1. **Remember to pray** with smart, customizable reminders
2. **Track spiritual progress** through detailed prayer logs and sessions
3. **Celebrate answered prayers** with anniversary notifications
4. **Stay private and secure** with full CloudKit encryption
5. **Pray together** by sharing prayer lists with family and churches

---

## Key Differentiators

- **Privacy-First**: Zero analytics, full encryption, no tracking
- **Spiritual Focus**: Designed for prayer (not generic task management)
- **Beautiful Design**: Modern SwiftUI with emoji customization
- **Cloud-Native**: Automatic iCloud sync across devices
- **Independent Creator**: Purpose-driven, not corporate
- **Smart Reminders**: Flexible cadence scheduling
- **Faith Community**: Collaborative prayer lists
- **Home Integration**: Widget and quick actions
