Little Quotes Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Little Quotes does not collect your data. Quotes, photos, and context live on your devices and sync privately via iCloud—no analytics, no ads, no trackers. Your family’s memories stay within your shared circle. Titles, notes, and images are never sold or shared.

Requirements: iOS 17.0 or later with an iCloud account for sync and sharing features.
