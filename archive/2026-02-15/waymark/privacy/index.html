Privacy

Waymark Prayer does not collect any of your data.

All lists, prayers, images, and notes are stored on your device and backed up to your private Apple iCloud account using CloudKit. All prayer title, notes, list names, list notes, answered prayer notes, and images are encrypted with your private Apple encryption key.

Waymark Prayer does not display any advertisements, or include any trackers/analytics.
