Why Yapware

yapware (noun): software made by converting spoken thought directly into agentic work. Also: the resulting class of suspiciously complete things.

yapware.dev is the canonical home of the term — a definition, a methodology, and a growing wall of Certified Yapware™ built by people who talked their software into existence.

Yap

Say it, don't spray it. Speak the thing you want into a microphone and let the agent take it from there.

Shape

Tell your clanker where it's wrong. Iteration is just more yapping, with feeling.

Ship

It's fine. Just ship it. Yapware that never ships is only yap.

Yap it. Ship it. Then read the writing for what building this way actually teaches you.
