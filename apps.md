  <div class="philosophy-list">
    <div class="philosophy-item">
      <div class="philosophy-title">Native iOS Craft</div>
      <div class="philosophy-desc">Interfaces that feel unmistakably at home on Apple platforms — fluid gestures, crisp typography, and purposeful motion.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="philosophy-item">
      <div class="philosophy-title">Purposeful Tech</div>
      <div class="philosophy-desc">Every feature earns its place by helping people learn deeply, grow spiritually, or connect with their community.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="philosophy-item">
      <div class="philosophy-title">Scientific Foundation</div>
      <div class="philosophy-desc">Research-backed systems like spaced repetition and habit theory shape the product roadmap, not trend chasing.</div>
    </div>
    <div class="philosophy-item">
      <div class="philosophy-title">Privacy by Default</div>
      <div class="philosophy-desc">Respect for user data is table stakes — sensible defaults, transparent controls, and sync you can trust.</div>
