# Waymark App - Executive Summary for Showcase Page

## Quick Overview

**Waymark** is a beautifully designed iOS prayer companion app that helps Christians build consistent prayer habits, track spiritual progress, celebrate answered prayers, and share their faith journey with others.

---

## What Problem Does Waymark Solve?

Many Christians struggle with:
- **Remembering to pray consistently** for important people and situations
- **Tracking their spiritual progress** and prayer life
- **Recognizing and celebrating** when God answers prayers
- **Finding a private, secure space** for their personal prayer practice
- **Praying together** with family and faith community

Waymark solves all of these with a prayer-specific app (not a generic task manager).

---

## Core Value Proposition

> "Your companion for building a consistent and meaningful prayer life."

Waymark helps users stay connected with God through regular prayer and remembrance of His faithfulness.

## Key Features at a Glance

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| Prayer Lists & Organization | Organize prayers by life area (family, health, work) with custom icons and colors |
| Smart Reminders | Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly) with customizable times |
| Prayer Sessions | Guided prayer experience with optional timer and progress tracking |
| Answered Prayers Tracking | Record and celebrate God's faithfulness with text and media |
| Anniversary Notifications | Milestone reminders (1 week, 1 month, 1 year) celebrate faith journey |
| Cloud Sync | Automatic iCloud sync across all your Apple devices |
| Prayer Sharing | Share prayer lists with family and faith community |
| Home Screen Widget | Prayer prompts and quick access from lock screen |
