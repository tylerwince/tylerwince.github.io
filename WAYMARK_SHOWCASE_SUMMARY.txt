PROBLEM SOLVED
================================================================================

Waymark solves the fundamental challenge many Christians face: maintaining a
consistent prayer practice while managing multiple prayer intentions. It:

1. Helps users REMEMBER TO PRAY through smart, customizable reminders
2. Enables users to TRACK SPIRITUAL PROGRESS through detailed prayer logs
3. Celebrates ANSWERED PRAYERS to strengthen faith and gratitude
4. Provides PRIVATE, SECURE SPACE for personal spiritual practice
5. Facilitates SHARED PRAYER with family, friends, and faith communities

CORE VALUE PROPOSITION
================================================================================

"Let Waymark be your spiritual waypoint, marking the journey of your 
relationship with God."

The app recognizes prayer as a spiritual discipline that requires consistency,
accountability, and celebration. Unlike generic task managers, Waymark is
specifically designed for the unique needs of prayer practice.

CORE DATA ENTITIES
================================================================================

1. PrayerList
   - Organizing container with icon and color
   - Customizable for different prayer areas
   
2. PrayerItem
   - Individual prayers with status and attachments
   - Text, notes, photos/videos
   
3. PrayerLog
   - Historical record of when prayers were prayed
   - Timestamps and notes
   
4. PrayerSchedule
   - Scheduling configuration for prayer reminders
   - Cadence and timing settings
   
5. PrayerSession
   - Group prayer session tracking
   - Duration and participant logging
   
6. UserPrayerSettings
   - User preferences and configuration

ALL ENTITIES SUPPORT:
- CloudKit synchronization
- Full text encryption
- Binary attachments
- Cloud storage
- UUID identification
- Timestamps
