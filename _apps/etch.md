## What is Etch?

**Etch is a native iOS spaced repetition flashcard app**‚Äîwhat Anki would have been if developed by Apple. It uses scientifically-proven learning techniques to help you retain information for longer periods with less study time.

The app automatically schedules your reviews at optimal intervals using the FSRS (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler) algorithm, ensuring you study at the perfect moment to remember information for a lifetime.

## Key Features

### Smart Spaced Repetition
Etch uses the advanced FSRS algorithm to intelligently schedule your flashcard reviews. The system adapts to your performance in real-time, adjusting difficulty based on how well you remember each card.

### AI-Powered Learning
- **AI Insights**: Automatically generates study summaries and related flashcards from your material
- **Multimodal Chat**: Discuss your study content with Gemini AI using text, images, and PDFs
- **Smart Card Generation**: Create flashcards instantly from existing content

### Flexible Card Types
- **Forward cards**: Traditional one-way learning
- **Bidirectional cards**: Learn both directions (e.g., English ‚Üî Spanish)
- **Quote Mode**: Memorize verbatim text with intelligent first-letter hints

### Beautiful Organization
- **Stack System**: Group flashcards with custom colors and SF Symbol icons
- **Full-Text Search**: Find any card or insight instantly
- **Analytics**: Track your progress with detailed metrics, charts, and streak tracking

### Native iOS Integration
- **Home Screen & Lock Screen Widgets**: Quick access to prayer prompts throughout the day
- **Siri Shortcuts**: Automate card creation with voice commands
- **iPad & Mac Support**: Optimized split-view layouts for larger screens
- **iCloud Sync**: Keep your cards synchronized across all devices

### Import Your Existing Decks
- Import from **Anki** (.apkg files)
- Import from **Obsidian** notes
- Export to CSV or JSON for backup

## The Science Behind It

Spaced repetition is backed by decades of cognitive psychology research. By reviewing information at gradually increasing intervals, you can:
- **Study less, remember more**
- **Counteract the forgetting curve**
- **Build long-term retention**
- **Save time and effort**

Etch's FSRS algorithm is the most advanced scheduling system available, providing even better results than traditional spaced repetition implementations.

## Privacy Policy

**Etch Flashcards does not collect any of your data.**

All stacks, flashcards, and study information is stored on your device and backed up to your private Apple iCloud account using [CloudKit](https://developer.apple.com/icloud/cloudkit/).

Etch Flashcards does not display any advertisements, or include any trackers/analytics.
