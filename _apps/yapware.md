## Why Yapware

**yapware** (noun): software made by converting spoken thought directly into agentic work. Also: the resulting class of suspiciously complete things.

[yapware.dev](https://yapware.dev) is the canonical home of the term — a definition, a methodology, and a growing wall of Certified Yapware™ built by people who talked their software into existence.
